Bronn

Olde English Bulldogge | 4.5 years old | Lovable

Woof Woof!

Hello, my name is Bronn. I'm an Olde English Bulldogge born in Mexico. I was adopted by my humans on August 19, 2017 when I was just 1.5 years old. My mantra: "Eat, sleep, poop, repeat."

Breed

People often stare at me confused because I look like an English Bulldog or take a guess that I'm an American Bulldog. Understandably so, because my breed was dervied from both, along with Bullmastiff and American Pit Bull Terrier. Learn more about my breed here.

Sleep

My absolute favorite thing in the world, other than engulf a whole plate of dog food, is to sleep! I've been told that when I do, I can shake a whole room with how loud I snore. It's been compared to a motoroboat engine starting. I can't help it, it's who I am! Also, butt rubs are to die for.

Play & Travel

My favorite park is Marymoor Off Leash Dog Park! I try to run and keep up with the other dogs but they're all so fast. My sprint can last for about 10 seconds before I have to stop and catch a breath. They must all be athletes or something because I just don't get it.

I tag along my humans' adventures. Believe it or not, I've hiked up Rattlesnake Ledge with an elevation gain of 1,175 ft! People at the top were impressed. I've travelled to many national parks: Sequoia National Park, Yosemite National Park, and Olympic National Park. I've even crossed the border to British Columbia, Canada to visit my aunts!
